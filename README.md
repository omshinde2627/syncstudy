SyncStudy

SyncStudy is a collaborative study platform designed to help students stay focused by connecting them with peers who are studying the same subject at the same time. The platform transforms studying from an isolated activity into a structured and accountable group experience.

Many students struggle with distractions, poor study environments, and lack of motivation when studying alone. SyncStudy solves this problem by intelligently matching students into small study groups based on their subject, exam type, and study duration. This creates a focused environment where students feel socially accountable and motivated to complete their study sessions.

The platform also includes productivity tracking through a Focus Score, which measures engagement using study duration, session participation, and test activity. Students can view their progress through a clean analytics dashboard that displays study hours, streaks, session counts, and performance insights.

Additionally, SyncStudy integrates subject-based test series, allowing students to evaluate their understanding and track improvement over time. By combining real-time collaboration, performance analytics, and structured study sessions, the platform aims to improve study consistency and learning outcomes.

Key Features

Smart student matchmaking based on subject and study goals

Small focused study groups (5–10 students)

Focus Score system to track productivity

Study analytics dashboard with performance insights

Integrated test series for practice and evaluation

Clean and distraction-free study interface

Technology Stack

TypeScript for application logic and development

CSS for responsive and modern UI design

PostgreSQL for data storage

PL/pgSQL for database functions and advanced queries

Goal

The goal of SyncStudy is to create a collaborative digital study environment where students can stay consistent, accountable, and productive while preparing for exams or academic goals.
